TEAM

  • Gaurav Pant

    An IIT Delhi Graduate, but useless, more or less, for any kind of job other than storytelling. When it comes to storytelling, whether via a photograph or a film, he surely knows his deal. Has a very unique style and vision towards filmmaking. Director of  3 shorts, 1 commercial and a few viral videos you must have seen on Internet. The journey for him has just begun, the dream has just started.

  • Deep Dakshay

    Photographer at heart, this man knows how to paint a frame. The aesthetics, the lighting, the composition and the beauty, he learned his way to breathe life into picture. Active participant of the Internet age, he has worked on several videos and viral content and not just as a photographer but also as a director. A complete machine when it comes to filmmaking.

  • Umang Srivastava

    After graduating from IIT Delhi he knew one thing for sure, he is not made up for 9 to 5 jobs. Writers are destined for a lifetime job. The best quality an artist can possess is to tell something extraordinary from, say, ordinary and this weirdo here has it. His rooted approach towards scriptwriting got him opportunity to work with giants like Dibakar Banerjee and Kanu Behl. We can shake this vending machine till a story drops out

  • Avinash Verma

    A MICA graduate, a writer and the one who can deal with all the bhasad. His critical writings on the movies and TV serials gained him a reputation not only among the national but international audiences. His work spans from MoifightClub to MAMI. As a senior figure of the team he is at the forefront of our all decision making.

  • Nitesh Verma

    This theatre actor has worked in short films, Internet videos and of course a lot of theatre. The most hardworking fellow of the lot, the person with best people skills. A person you can assign any task and he won't disappoint you, you can simply rely on him for anything to everything. This is the guy who gets the shit done.

  • Yashveer Rana

    NIT Rourkela, Computer Science, Hacker, great communication skills... these things are sufficient for someone to lead a life of comfort and luxury. But alas, passion hits hard.

    Now a Musician, Composer, Sound designer and name everything that goes into your ear as a sound waveform. One stop solution of our acoustics requirements.
